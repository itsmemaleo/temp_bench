🎧 BAEmpBench

Benchmarking Audio-Based Empathetic Response Judgment in Large Audio Language Models

📊 Dataset Overview

  • Total dialogues: 5,436
  • Gold-standard samples: 379 (human-annotated)
  • Datasets: Anno-MI (therapy), MESC (scripted), AvaMERG (synthetic)
  • Six empathy dimensions: Vocal Warmth, Perceived Genuineness, Prosodic Attunement, Emotional Reaction, Exploration, Interpretation

🔊 Sample Audio with Annotations

Example 1: Anno-MI (Therapy Session)

Context: "Do you have a plan as to where you might want to go?"
Speaker: "Um, I'm not sure."
Listener: "So, kind of keeping your options open."

Annotations: Emotional Reaction: 1 Exploration: 0 Interpretation: 2 Genuineness: 1 Prosody: 2 Warmth: 1

Example 2: MESC (Scripted Therapy)

Context: "You wanna write everybody's story for them?"
